Bun Lays

Here is a quick recipe to make a delicious Bun Lays!!

Ingredients

The ingredients required for preparing a Bun Lays are:

  • Bun - 1
  • Orange Lays - small packet
  • Mayonnise
  • Cheese
  • Onion - 1/2 small
  • Butter - To Toast

Steps to prepare Bun Lays

Follow these steps to make a delicious Bun Lays

  1. Cut the bun in 2 halves.
  2. Apply mayonnise on each side.
  3. Add chopped onions to 1 side of the bun
  4. Add crushed orange flavoured lays to the other side of the bun.
  5. Add grated cheese on any side of the bun.
  6. Now apply butter to the pan and toast the bun on each side for 2 mins each.
  7. Now serve the bun lays on a plate with a side of mayonnise.

Enjoy your Delicious Bun Lays:)
