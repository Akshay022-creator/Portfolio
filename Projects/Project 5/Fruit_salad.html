Fruit Salad

Here is a quick recipe to make a delicious Fruit Salad!!

Ingredients

The ingredients required for preparing a Fruit Salad are:

  • Apple - 1
  • Mango - 1
  • Strawberry - 4
  • Banana - 2
  • Pineapple - 1
  • Papaya - 1
  • Cashew - 4
  • Grapes - 8
  • Raisins - 4
  • Vanilla Ice cream - 1 scoop
  • Honey - 1 Tbsp

Steps to prepare Fruit Salad

Follow these steps to make a Fresh Fruit salad

  1. Cut apple mango and papaya into cubes and put it in a bowl.
  2. Cut pineapple, strawberries and bananas into slices and put in the bowl.
  3. Put grapes into the bowl.
  4. Add crushed cashews and raisins into the bowl.
  5. Add 1 Tbsp of hiney itno the bowl for sweetness.
  6. Add 1 scoop of vanilla ice cream and serve it chill in a bowl.

Enjoy your fresh Fruit Salad :)
