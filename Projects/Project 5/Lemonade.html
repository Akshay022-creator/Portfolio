Lemonade

Here is a quick recipe to make lemonade on a hot day!!

Ingredients

The ingredients required for preparing a lemonade are:

  • Lemons-2 medium sized
  • Sugar - 4 Tbsp
  • Salt - A pinch
  • Cardamom - 2 pods
  • Water - 1 glass
  • Ice Cubes - optional

Steps to prepare lemonade

Follow these steps to make a perfect lemonade

  1. Put water in a glass.
  2. Now squeeze the lemons into the glass of water(remove the seeds)
  3. Add a pinch of salt to the glass.
  4. Now add 4 tbsp of sugar into the glass.
  5. Crush the cardamom pods and add it into the glass
  6. Now stir the mixture and serve with ice cubes

Enjoy your lemonade :)
