American Sweet Corn

Here is a quick recipe to make American Sweet Corn street style!!

Ingredients

The ingredients required for preparing a Sweet Corn are:

  • Boiled Sweet Corn - 1 cuo
  • Salt - as per taste
  • Pepper - 1/2 tsp
  • Chaat masala - 1/2 Tsp
  • Lemon juice - 1/2 Tsp
  • Butter - 1 Tsp

Steps to prepare American Sweet Corn

Follow these steps to make a tangy sweet corn

  1. Boil 1 cup of corn and add it in a bowl.
  2. Put 1/2 Tsp of pepper powder.
  3. Add 1/2 tsp of Chaat masala.
  4. Add salt as per taste.
  5. Add 1 Tsp of melted butter to give it an additional taste (optional)
  6. Squeeze a bit of lemon juice to make it tangy
  7. Mix all the ingredients well and serve it in a bowl.

Enjoy your tangy street style Sweet Corn :)
